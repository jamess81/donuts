About Us

When pet owners are facing difficult financial circumstances, Pawz In Need is there to provide food for animals that might otherwise have to be rehomed or abandoned. Keeping people together with their beloved pets is important to us. You can read articles about our work in The Wirral Globe and Liverpool Echo.

We are a volunteer organisation that relies on contributions from the general public. You can help today either by making a donation to our Paypal account, or giving food directly at one of five drop-off points around the Wirral. You can be sure we greatly appreciate any help for local pets in urgent need.
